Crafted by Tradition, Backed by Experience

With over 40 years of experience, Gwent Lime specialises in traditional lime plastering, eco-friendly solutions and contemporary finishes. From period to modern buildings, large-scale commercial restorations to private, boutique residential projects, from South Wales to UK-wide, Gwent Lime offers the professional touch right for you. Our speciality of heritage restoration, whether it's a listed farmhouse or estate, means that we have a keen eye for preserving the fabric and character of old buildings using breathable and historically fitting materials. Our approach ensures the long-term health and beauty of your project, preserving its legacy for generations to come. In addition to restoration work, we also collaborate on eco-conscious new builds and contemporary interiors, offering luxury polished finishes such as Venetian and Tadelakt plaster. Our work combines deep craft knowledge with a modern awareness of sustainability, performance, and timeless aesthetics. Whether it's conserving a 17th-century wall or adding a sleek natural finish to a modern bathroom, every project is approached with care, integrity, and attention to detail.
